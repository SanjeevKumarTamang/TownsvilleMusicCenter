Goals

The website is intended to increase membership for Townsville Community Music Centre and escalate their engagement in different activities like volunteering for administration, organising and promoting concerts and workshops.The website should assist to increase ticket sales especially among the general public and encourage performers to let Music center organize concert for them meanwhile encouraging them to cooperate with promotion, photo shoots, media releases, etc.

Success Evaluation

The Success of the wesite would be evaluated from following points.

1) The acceleration in the membership after the website has been hosted
2) The volume of members participating in the events
3) Ticket sales statistics
4) Increase in the number of requests for organizing events or concerts
5) Participation of the performers in promotion, photo shoots and media releases.

Target Audience

The website is targeted to appeal people from wide range of styles and genres therefore includes wide range of ages and tastes. Involvement of children is predominated by the parents or the family members.
