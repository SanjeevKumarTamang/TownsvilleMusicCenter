The website is intended to increase membership for Townsville Community Music Centre and escalate their engagement in different activities like volunteering for administration, organising and promoting concerts and workshops. The website should assist to increase ticket sales especially among the general public and encourage performers to let Music center organize concert for them .The website should encourage them to cooperate with promotion, photo shoots, media releases, etc.
